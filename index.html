Biography

SeongJae Park is a programmer who loves to analyze and develop systems. He received a PhD in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Seoul National University in 2019. His research interests include operating systems, parallel computing, and memory management. He is also participating in the Linux kernel community just for fun and profit.
